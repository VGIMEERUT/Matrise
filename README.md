UP B.Ed Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2026 registration is currently underway, offering aspiring teachers a pathway to pursue a two-year B.Ed program for the 2026-28 session. Conducted by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi, this exam facilitates admissions across Uttar Pradesh colleges.

Key Dates

[UP B.Ed Registration 2026](https://matrise.in/blog/up-b-ed-entrance-exam-2026-dates-fees-registration-syllabus-result/) began on February 10, 2026, with the deadline for submission without late fees set for March 5, 2026. Applications with late fees can be submitted until March 10, 2026, while the exam is tentatively scheduled for April 20-25, 2026.

Admit cards are expected in early April, followed by results and counseling processes.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates need a graduation or postgraduate degree with at least 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST categories) from a recognized university. The exam targets graduates aiming for school teaching roles.

Application Process

Visit bujhansi.ac.in and select "New Registration" to enter your name, date of birth, email, and mobile number. Upon submission, receive login credentials via email/SMS, then complete the form, upload documents, and pay the fee online.

Fees and Payment

The fee is approximately ₹1,400-₹1,650 for General/OBC and ₹850 for SC/ST, payable online. A correction window opens in March for form edits.

Exam Overview

The entrance test covers General Knowledge, Language, and Teaching Aptitude. Qualified candidates proceed to counseling for seat allocation in participating colleges.
